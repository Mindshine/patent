Protection of Industrial Designs in Ukraine

Industrial design-result of the creative activity of a person in the field of artistic designing. Industrial design meets requirements for patentability if it is novel and industrially applicable. Applicant-person who has filed an application for te grant of a patent. In the capacity of such a person may be inventor, his heir, employer, a successor of the inventor or employer. Patentis granted without examination for 10 years from the application filing date at the State Patent Office. At the request of the holder a patent may be prolonged for not more than 5 years. Publicationis in Official Bulletin of the State Patent Office. Grant of patentis carried out within one month from publication in the Official Bulletin. Patent iss granted on its holder's own responsibility without guarantee of validity. Protestagainst the grant of a patent may be raised before the date of publication of the information about granting a patent

In order to obtain a patent of Ukraine for industrial designs, foreigners and non-residents should submit:

  • Order-letter containing:
    • applicant's name, citizenship/nationality and address/domicile (individual/juridical person);
    • inventor's name, citizenship and address/domicile
    • title of industrial design;
    • the claimed convention priority date (if any), country and number of basic application.
  • Description of the industrial design.
  • A set of photographs of the design.
  • Drawings, schemes, maps (if neccessary).
  • Power of Attorney.
  • Certified copy of basic application field in a Paris Convention Contracting State (when claiming priority) or document that confirms an industrial design's demonstration at the exhibitions on the territory of the Paris Convention Contracting State.
