The ukrainian trademark registration practice

Applicantcan be any juridical or individual a bona fide proprietor undertaking industrial or rendering service Termthe registration is valid for 10 years. The term can be prolonged every time for not more than 10 years. Useobligatory. Registration can be cancelled in case of non-using the trademark within 3 years from the date of registration. Markingcompulsory Licenselicense agreement, assignment agreement. Assignmentfor exclusive use of the trademark and assignment agreement for registration of the trademark have to be registered in the State Patern Office of Ukraine Service are under legislation for trademarks. Marks Goodsare classified according to the International classification of the goods and services.

To register a foreign trademark in Ukraine it is neccessary to submit:

  • Order-letter containing:
    • applicant's name and domicile/address;
    • list of goods for which a trademark is to be registered and classes of goods according to the International classification of goods and services.
  • Image of the trademark - as JPG-file
  • description of the trademark (if neccessary)
  • Power of Attorney
  • Certified copy of basic application (when claiming priority)
