Protection of the rights of Inventions and Utility Models in Ukraine

Invention(utility model)result of the creative activity of a person in any field of technology.
Requirements for patent abilitynovelty, inventive step and industrially applicability.
Applicantperson who has filed an application for the grant of patent. In the capacity of such a person may be inventor, his heir, employer, a successor of the inventor or employer.
Patent for inventionis granted for 20 years from the application filing date at the State Patent Office. A request for examination may be filled within 3 years from the application filing date in Ukraine.
Patent for utility modelis granted for 10 years from the application filing date at the State Patent Office.
Publicationis in Official Bulleting of the State Patent Office.
Grant of patentis carried out within one month from publication in the Official Bulletin.
Protestagainst the grant of a patent may be raised before the Board of Appeal within 6 months from the date of publication of the information about granting a patent

In order to obtain a patent of Ukraine for invention (utility model), foreigners and non-resident should submit:

  • Order-letter containing:
    • applicant's name, citizenship/nationality and address/domicile (individual/juridical person);
    • inventor's name, citizenship and address/domicile
    • title of invention and its class according to the International Classification of Inventions;
    • the claimed convention priority date (if any), country and number of basic application.
  • Description of the invention.
  • Claims of the invention.
  • Drawings and/or other illustrated materials (if they are referred to in the description).
  • Abstract.
  • Power of Attorney.
  • Certified copy of basic application field in a Paris Convention Contracting State (when claiming priority).
  • For a pct application you should submit a cover page of the PCT-publication.
