About Us

The company is established in February, 1998. Director, Larisa B. Baroncha, is the patent attorney of the Ukraine (registration No. 134). The director of the company is the licensed engineer of an electronic technology, has higher professional education in protection of intellectual property and experience of activity in this area more than 20 years and is a member of All-Ukrainian association of the patent attorneys and International association of the protection of industrial property (AIPPI). She has experience both with national, and with foreign applicant. The company disposes the specialists in different areas of science and engineering, for example, electrical engineering and electronic technology, engine and motor industry, building stuffs and designs, architecture, medicine, chemistry etc.
